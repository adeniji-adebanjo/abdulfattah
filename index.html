Steps to prepare a delicious Amala by Chef Kabirat

  1. Boil water
  2. Sieve Yam/cassava flour
  3. Pour the flour gently into the water and stir
  4. Keep turning while the pot is in between the legs and add more flour till it solidifies
  5. Add little water to cook well
